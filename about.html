About Me

Personal Life

I am a hobbyist photographer and adventurer. I enjoy finding creative ways to link different outdoor sports and modes of human powered transportation, and showcasing the beauty of the places my adventures take me.

Given that my adventures are taking me to increasingly remote and beautiful areas, I am currently focusing on landscape and nude photography. I love finding creative ways to incorporate the human form in its most natural state with a wild, seldom visited atmosphere.

Professional Life

I am currently studying psychology at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill where I continue to hone my outdoor expertise and work to find my artistic direction through photography.

In the future, I plan to study cyberpsychology in order to find more humane ways to relate to technology that all too easily rules ones life.
