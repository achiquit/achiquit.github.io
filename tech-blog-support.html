Why I Do It

I've been helping a handful of clients since I was 18 years old. They have come and gone, but I have been on-call for any technology issue at 2-3 households for the last 7 years. I have always relished giving back to the community and helping those who are not as knowledgable about technology.
Now, I'm in school full time studying Psychology and Information Science, and I need a way to put gas in the tank and food in my belly!

Prices

For the last 7 years I have operated on a need-based sliding scale, and I do not intend to change that. The most I would be willing to accept is $50/hr, but if that is out of reach for your budget, pay what you can spare, even if that's just a homemade muffin.
