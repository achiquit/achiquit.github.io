Milk Tag Collection

This is an ongoing collection of photographs taken in the piedmont region of North Carolina, primarily in Chapel Hill and Durham. They were taken anywhere between 2017 and the present.

I was inspired to document these tags in particular because I saw them frequently in my urban wanderings. They stood out from other tags because of the variety of styles used, creative placements, and their ubiquity.
